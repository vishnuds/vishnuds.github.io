It's not easy being a cat. Sure, people think it's all sunshine and fur balls, but it turns out humans just have no idea.

Meet Alan. He's the outrageously lazy housecat who won't let anything get in the way of his tummy tickles and his delicious, meaty cat food. But, when Alan's world is turned upside down, he finds himself needing to fight for purr-vival.

In this brilliant debut, Smith opens our eyes to the troubled world of our feline friends, and shows us just why we need to listen to them when it comes to life's biggest problems. Alan's hilarious and totally honest journey from no-moggy to top cat teaches us just how important it is to believe in yourself, and never get caught with your tail between your legs.
